Privacy Policy

ScanPro — Last updated: March 2026

Overview

ScanPro is a document scanning app developed by Tushar Lama. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Collect

Authentication Data
When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo URL. This is stored securely in Firebase Authentication and Firestore.

Device Information
Upon sign-in, we store basic device details: device model, iOS version, app version, locale, and timezone. This helps us diagnose issues and improve the app.

Analytics
We collect anonymous usage analytics (screen views, feature usage, scan counts) through Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and improve it. No document content is ever transmitted.

Advertising
ScanPro uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access, read, or upload your scanned documents
  • We do not collect OCR text results
  • We do not access your photo library beyond images you explicitly select
  • We do not sell any personal data to third parties

Data Storage

All scanned documents are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share or export them.

Authentication and profile data is stored in Google Firebase (servers in the United States).

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication — Sign-in and user management
  • Firebase Firestore — User profile storage
  • Firebase Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics
  • Google AdMob — Advertising

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data by signing out in the app's Settings. To request complete data deletion from our servers, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

ScanPro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
