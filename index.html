Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my scanned documents stored?

All documents are stored locally on your device. They never leave your phone unless you explicitly share or export them.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your documents are protected by your device's built-in encryption and passcode. We never upload your scans to any server.

What happens if I delete the app?

Deleting the app will permanently remove all locally stored documents. Make sure to export anything important before uninstalling.

How do I export a document as PDF?

Open any document, tap the share button, and choose "Export as PDF". You can also use the Tools tab to convert documents.

Can I remove ads?

Premium features including ad removal will be available in a future update.

The OCR text isn't accurate. What can I do?

OCR works best with clear, well-lit scans. Try re-scanning the document with better lighting and ensure the text is in focus.
